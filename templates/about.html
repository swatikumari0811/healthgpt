About HealthBot

HealthBot is an AI-powered health assistant designed to provide symptom-based suggestions, recommend nearby hospitals and pharmacies, and offer instant healthcare support through a user-friendly chatbot interface. It aims to make basic medical assistance accessible anytime, anywhere.

Meet the Developer

Swati Kumari is a passionate full-stack developer from Ranchi, Jharkhand. She developed HealthBot using Python, Flask, MySQL, and Dialogflow with a mission to simplify access to health services through intelligent automation and user-centric design.
